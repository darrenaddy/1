About MMC:

Makers of Triad Machines

Our Founder: Jim Merrick

Jim L. Merrick, is considered on of the three pioneers in the innovation of automated machines for the building industry. MMC started in Jim's filling station garage, but quickly outgrew it and moved to a dedicated facility. Jim died in 2002 with his legacy established, and the stage set for the continued growth of his company into the future.

2nd Generation: Diversification

His son, Rick Merrick, saw to not only to the growth, but the diversification of the businesses. Consequently, Merrick Machine Company is just one of the Merrick Family of businesses today. Today, Rick is the company's Chairman Emeritus, and is having a ball with the Merrick Auto Museum - now home to the countries largest collection of vintage automobile ephemera.

3rd Generation: Unprecedented Sales

Jim's grandson, Russ Merrick, is not your "typical" CEO: You are likely to find Russ "pitching in" on the MMC production floor - assisting whenever he sees a production bottleneck beginning to occur. His leadership inspires everyone who works for today's companies. The result? Merrick Machine Co. is setting business records. Yet we know that our business future is based on making YOUR business more successful.
